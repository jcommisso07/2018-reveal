Hook and Winch

The hook and winch system allows for flexibility when climbing. To climb, we simply deploy the hook by lifting the elevator and intake system, and rapidly move out of the way to allow another robot to deploy. The powerful winch is able to lift our robot in seconds.

Intake

The intake system consists of two compliant wheels mounted to spring loaded arms. This allows crates to be picked up from all orientations. The intake system is able to shoot crates at angles and completely fold in.
